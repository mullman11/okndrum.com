Black and Red OKN Strips

The OKN Drum Pro App features various modes, including modes with black and red stripes. Users have the ability to adjust the stripe size by using a pinch gesture.

Pediatric OKN Drum

The OKN Drum Pro comes equipped with a range of pediatric fixation targets, featuring eye-catching cartoons of animals, puppies, and dragons designed to hold children's attention.

Metronome Tool

The OKN Drum Pro includes a metronome feature to assist with timing during exercises.

Tools and Features

  • Black and Red Stripe Modes
  • Adjustable Stripe Width Using Pinch Gesture
  • Multiple Speeds
  • Adjust Speed Using Plus/Minus Buttons or the Dropdown Menu
  • Metronome to Assist in Timing Exercises
  • Multiple Fixation Targets Including Animals, Puppies, Dinosaurs, and Shapes
  • Compatible with iPhone and iPad
  • Turn Device to Change the Direction of the Stripes, uses Gyroscope to Maintain Pediatric Targets in Correct Orientation
